How we project ROI

Conservative by design.

Every projection on this page is built from real platform data — historical view rates, audience-market splits, last-90-day engagement. No vanity numbers. We'd rather under-promise and give your CMO a clean win than inflate.

Reach
∑ followers
Sum of follower counts across all selected talents and platforms. Verified within 14 days.
Impressions
reach × view-rate
Industry average view rates: Reels 12-18%, TikTok 8-12%, Twitch concurrents.
Audience match
market %
Platform audience-market data: KSA, MENA, Global. Reported per-talent in our roster.
Watch time
avg × duration
Avg concurrent viewers × stream length × ad-position multiplier. Pre-roll = 1.0×, mid = 0.7×.
