№ 04 · 12-month embedded

Day-in-the-Life
Content Engine.

Six to eight charismatic talents — esports influencers plus a handful of player faces — sell the brand a 12-month embedded presence. The brand becomes a recurring fixture in monthly behind-the-scenes content, bootcamp footage, travel vlogs. Falcons produces centrally; players just live their lives. The marginal cost of a 14th brand showing up in B-roll is effectively zero.

How operational leverage compounds

The lower the lift,
the higher the margin.

These five aren't ranked by price — they're ranked by how much the activation runs without us. № 04 is the highest-margin product on the menu because once the embedded relationship is set up, every additional brand showing up in B-roll costs us almost nothing to deliver. № 01 is closer behind it. Brands who understand this scale spend with us. Brands who don't, churn.
